Day 2 focused on understanding platform architecture concepts.

Topics studied:

• Azure Cloud Adoption Framework  
• the "Ready" methodology

The key idea is that cloud architecture is not only about deploying resources.

Architects design environments that support:

• scalability  
• governance  
• operational reliability  

## CAF Core Concepts Summary

Landing Zones
    The foundational environment where workloads are deployed, including governance, identity, networking, and structure.

Governance
    Rules and controls such as policies, RBAC, and budgets that ensure safe and consistent cloud usage.

Resource Organization
    The way resources are grouped and structured, typically using resource groups, naming conventions, and tags.

Subscription Strategy
    How cloud environments are separated for cost, access, and risk management, often by dev/test/prod.

Management Groups
    A hierarchy used to organize subscriptions and apply governance at scale.
